Magic vs Celtics recap: Boston flipped the game in one quarter.

Boston beat Orlando 113-108 at TD Garden. The clean story is the Celtics' 42-point third quarter, but the box score has more texture: Paolo Banchero had a triple-double, Orlando won the glass, and Boston still survived by owning the three-point and turnover math.

The short version

Orlando led by nine at halftime after a 32-point second quarter. Boston answered with 42 in the third, turned Magic mistakes into points, and closed a tight regular-season finale despite scoring only 19 in the fourth.

The number I kept coming back to was not just the final score. It was Boston's 19 made threes against Orlando's 12, plus a 23-11 edge in points off turnovers.
